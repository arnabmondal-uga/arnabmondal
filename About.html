About

I was born and raised in Durgapur (West Bengal, India). I attended the West Bengal University of Technology (aka MAKAUT) for my undergraduate degree in Biotechnology. I studied biotechnology because of an interest in working at the intersection of engineering and biology. During my master’s research, I worked on environmental engineering to focus on bioremediation of crude oil sludge. However, I transitioned back to biomaterials research during my Ph.D. and successfully applied microbiological techniques learned during my master’s research. My passion remains with developing biomaterials and significantly leverage biomaterials-based therapeutics to solve modern-day healthcare problems.
