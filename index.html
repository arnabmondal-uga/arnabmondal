Arnab Mondal

Engineering Ph.D. Candidate at the School of Chemical, Materials and Biomedical Engineering, University of Georgia.

My Name is Arnab Mondal, and I am an Engineering Ph.D. Candidate at the School of Chemical, Materials and Biomedical Engineering, University of Georgia. I work with Professor Hitesh Handa on nitric oxide (NO)-based translational research, developing bioinspired NO-producing materials with therapeutic potential. Specifically, my research is focused on improving the multifunctionality of NO-releasing polymer composite surfaces often used for medical device coatings to prevent infection or improve hemocompatibility.
